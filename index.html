What are Subway skaters ?

Subway skaters 3d are a collection of 2,222 unique generated misfits, rebels, dreamers, and troublemakers skateboards living on the Solana Blockchain. Inspired by retro art, music, and fashion, - Subway skaters perfectly blend fun, funky vibes with high-quality 3D art to embody a new cultural movement.
